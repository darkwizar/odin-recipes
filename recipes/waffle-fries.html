Waffle Fries

This is how you make some homemade waffle fries!

Ingrediants

  • 4 cups of ice cubes
  • 1 tbp of salt
  • 1 tbp of black pepper
  • 2 quarts of olive oil

How to make it!

  1. Fill a large bowl with 4 cups of ice and cold water.
  2. Scrub the potatoes under running cold water and pat dry with paper towels. USe a mandoline set to waffle cut the fries. After cut transfer to ice bath and let it chill for 15 to 30 minutes.
  3. Add the salt and black pepper to a bowl and stir to combine.
  4. Put 2 quarts of olive oil into the dutch oven. Let the potatoes dry.
  5. Add the fries in the oven to let them fry. Stir to prevent sticking.
  6. Take them out when they're browning.
  7. Take them out and let them sit. Sprinkle with salt and let them rest.
Then just enjoy!
