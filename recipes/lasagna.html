Lasagna

How to make some homemade lasagna!

Ingrediants

  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzaralla cheese
  • 1 tps olive oil
  • 1/2 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 lb. ground italian sausage
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • extra cheese (just in case)

Make the meat sauce

  1. Heat the olive oil and add the sauce in the pan. let it sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Add the beef and the sausage and increase the heat
  3. Let the sauce simmer

Combine the noodles with the sauce

  1. Spread the sauce in the pan. Next add the lasagna noodles
  2. Spread the cheese as you repeat the process
  3. Try to finish it to the top of the pan with all of the ingrediants

Bake

  1. Bake for 35 minutes. Remove the cover and bake for 10 more minutes.
  2. Remove and let it set for 15 minutes
Final step is to enjoy the food!
