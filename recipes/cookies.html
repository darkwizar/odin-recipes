Cookies

How to make some homemade cookies!

Ingrediants

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Flour

How to make it!

  1. Beat the butter,sugar and flour together until blended together
  2. form the dough into tiny balls and roll the sugar into them
  3. Bake them until you see them golden around the edges and bottom
  4. Let them rest and cool for 10 minutes

Then finally, enjoy them!
